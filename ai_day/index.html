AI in Mathematics and Mathematics Education Day

General Description

This single day workshop will be held on June 13th, 2025 at the Stevens Institute of Technology. The purpose of this workshop is to explore the now numerous applications of AI-methods to a variety of mathematical disciplines, as well as to mathematics education.

This workshop will act as the final day of GAGTA 2025 in the GAGTA (Geometric and Asymptotic Group Theory with Applications) series of conferences.
