AI and Mathematics: Current Trends and Future Directions

General Description

This single day workshop is part of an annual series that is shared between Bar-Ilan University, CalTech, and Stevens Institute of Technology. This year's meeting will be held on June 13th, 2025 at Stevens Institute of Technology. The purpose of this workshop is to explore the now numerous applications of AI-methods to a variety of mathematical disciplines, as well as to mathematics education.

This year's workshop will also act as the final day of GAGTA 2025 in the GAGTA (Geometric and Asymptotic Group Theory with Applications) series of conferences.
