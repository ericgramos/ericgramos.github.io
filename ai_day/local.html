AI and Mathematics: Current Trends and Future Directions

The conference will be held at Stevens Institute of Technology, in Hoboken, New Jersey. The conference meetings will be held in the Babbio Center, room 104. See the map for directions.

Getting to Stevens

Stevens is located in Hoboken, New Jersey, directly across the Hudson River from Manhattan. The campus is easily accessible by public transportation, car, or taxi.
